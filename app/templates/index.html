What is a MovieFlow?
Movie flow is an opensource online platform for cinema lovers of any generation. Here you can find information about the best movies, write reviews, rank the movies, share your opinions and ideas with fellow movie lovers.
Our community.
It is a community of movie enthusiasts who interact with each other. Share your movies with friends and discover the movies they love. You can publish your watchlist by creating a “Flow”. The “Flow” is the list of movies that you create, you can either keep it private or share with other users to help them find new interesting movies.
What we provide?
To use Movie Flow just register and dive in. We provide more than 200000 movies for any taste. Search for the movies to watch, explore new genres, discover new actors, directors and many other giants of movie industry. All this you can do using Movie Flow.
